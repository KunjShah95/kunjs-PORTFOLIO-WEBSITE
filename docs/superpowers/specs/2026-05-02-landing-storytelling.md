# Landing Page Storytelling Redesign - Specification

**Date:** 2026-05-02  
**Status:** APPROVED  
**Type:** Homepage Redesign

---

## Overview

Transform the landing page into an emotional + storytelling experience that converts visitors into explorers.

**Goal:** Make people fall in love with your story and explore all pages.

---

## Visual Direction

### Color Palette (Existing)
- Primary: Deep Blue #1A3A5C
- Background: White/Off-white
- Use accent colors for CTAs

### Style Keywords
- Emotional, human, real
- Story-first, trust-second
- Curiosity-driven exploration

---

## Component: New Hero (Emotional Hook)

### Current Problem
Generic: "AI Engineer building agentic systems"

### Redesign
New hook (choose one):
```
Option A: "Every AI system fails. Until someone builds it right."
Option B: "I don't just build AI. I build AI that works in the real world."
Option C: "The best AI isn't the smartest. It's the one that solves problems."
```

### Layout
- Hook: 1-line emotional impact (top fold)
- Name + Title: Secondary
- Value proposition: Short, concrete
- CTA: "See my work" → projects
- Live metrics badge: Small, credible

---

## Component: Mini-Story Section

### Purpose
30-second read about your AI journey

### Content Categories
1. **The WHY** — What made you care about AI
2. **The HOW** — What you learned building
3. **The WHAT** — What you build now

### Format
- 3 short paragraphs
- Each 2-3 sentences max
- End with curiosity gap: "But that's just the beginning..."

---

## Component: Credibility Strip

### Purpose
Build trust quickly with metrics

### Metrics to Showcase
- 23% bias detected (EquityLens)
- 99.9% uptime
- 40% faster (SmartFlow)
- 11 total projects
- Live demos available

### Layout
Horizontal strip below story section
Icons + numbers + labels

---

## Component: Projects Teaser

### Purpose
Lead with EquityLens as flagship, hint at more

### Content
- Featured: EquityLens case study (problem + result only)
- Tagline: "11 projects. Each one solves a real problem."
- CTA: "Explore all →" + "Try live demos →"

---

## Component: Explore Teaser (per section)

### Purpose
Guide curious visitors to explore all pages

### Implementation
Each section ends with soft CTA:
- After Hero: "See my work →"
- After Projects summary: "Explore all 11 →"
- After Experience: "See my journey →"
- After Skills: "How I built this →"
- Before Contact: "Want to work together? →"

---

## Component: Scroll Curiosity

### Purpose
Keep visitors scrolling

### Implementation
- "See what I built ↓" scroller
- Progress indicator
- "X% of my work revealed" fun metric

---

## Implementation Priority

### Phase 1: Hero Hook
- New emotional hook
- Live metrics badge
- Clear CTA

### Phase 2: Story Section
- Mini-story component
- Credibility strip

### Phase 3: Explore CTAs
- Add teaser CTAs to each section
- Scroll curiosity

### Phase 4: Polish
- Animation timing
- Mobile optimization

---

## Success Criteria

- Visitors read the story (not bounce immediately)
- Click-through to /projects increases
- Visitor explores 2+ pages per session
- "This guy is different" reaction

---

## Notes

- Keep existing tech stack
- Maintain existing pages
- Don't change navigation
- Focus on content + CTAs only
